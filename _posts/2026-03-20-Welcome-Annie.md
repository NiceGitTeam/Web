NiceGit is thrilled to welcome [Annie Roys](/team/annie/) as our new CEO. Annie joins at an exciting stage in NiceGit's journey, bringing a sharp strategic mind and a wealth of experience leading high-growth organisations.

<!--more-->

![Annie Roys](/images/authors/annie-roys.jpg){: class="small" }

Annie comes to NiceGit with a distinguished background in pharmaceutical research, where she led teams navigating the complex intersection of rigorous science, regulatory environments, and commercial growth. That experience — demanding precision, clear communication, and long-term thinking — translates perfectly to the challenges of scaling a developer tools company.

As CEO, Annie will be driving NiceGit's strategic direction, growth initiatives, and commercial partnerships, working closely with the team to bring our vision of making Git accessible to every developer within reach.

Welcome, Annie — we're excited to have you at the helm!
